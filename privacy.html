Privacy Policy for Flora Gleam Pruning Services

At Flora Gleam Pruning, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or use our services. We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy rights in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide our services efficiently and improve your experience. The information collected includes but is not limited to:

  • Personal Identification Information: Such as your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and other contact details you voluntarily provide.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with our website, including device information, browser type, pages visited, and browsing patterns collected via cookies or similar technologies.
  • Service Information: Details about the services you request, quotes, inquiries, feedback, and communications with our team.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and manage our services efficiently and communicate with you regarding your service requests.
  • Improve our website, customer service, and marketing efforts to better meet your needs.
  • Send important notices, service updates, promotions, or administrative messages related to your account or services.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our or others’ rights, safety, or property.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We value your trust and do not sell your personal data. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: Trusted third parties who assist us in service delivery, website hosting, payment processing, or marketing.
  • For Legal Reasons: If required by law, regulation, or legal process to protect rights, safety, or compliance obligations.
  • Business Transfers: In case of mergers, acquisitions, or sales of assets, we may transfer your information as part of the transaction.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience, analyze site traffic, and provide tailored content. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings; however, disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Our website uses secure protocols, encryption, and access controls to safeguard your personal information during online transmission and storage.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. We encourage you to contact us to exercise these rights or if you have any questions about your personal information.

7. Retention of Data

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, abide by legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

8. International Data Transfers

Flora Gleam Pruning operates primarily in the USA. If your data is transferred outside of your country, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites for your convenience. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such websites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies prior to sharing any information.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are not directed at children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without parental consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or technological advances. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
