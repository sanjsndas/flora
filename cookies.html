Cookies Policy for Flora Gleam Pruning Services

Welcome to the Cookies Policy of Flora Gleam Pruning. This policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website to enhance your experience, provide personalized content, analyze site traffic, and ensure proper functionality. Our commitment to transparency and your privacy is paramount. Please read the details carefully to understand how these technologies affect your interaction with our digital services.

Introduction to Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They serve various purposes such as remembering your preferences, facilitating secure login, and collecting analytical data about site usage. Other tracking technologies include web beacons, pixels, and local storage that complement cookie functions. These tools help us deliver an efficient, secure, and customized online experience tailored to your preferences and needs.

Types of Cookies We Use

Our website employs several types of cookies, each serving distinct roles:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website’s core functionality, including navigation, security features, and form submissions. Without these cookies, certain features may not function properly.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: Collect anonymous information about how visitors use our website, which pages are popular, and any error messages encountered. This data helps us optimize and improve the user experience.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your choices, such as language preferences or region, to provide a more personalized experience on subsequent visits.
  • Advertising and Targeting Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads and measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns. These cookies may be set by us or third-party partners and enable customized advertising based on your browsing behavior.

How We Use Cookies

Cookies enable us to enhance the browsing experience by:

  • Remembering your login status and secure settings between sessions to save time during your return visits.
  • Collecting aggregated data that identifies trends and usage patterns, helping us prioritize improvements and bug fixes.
  • Facilitating social media integrations that allow you to share content or engage with our social platforms without repeatedly logging in.
  • Delivering personalized content and advertisements tailored to your interests as inferred from your interaction with our website and partner sites.

Third-Party Cookies and Tracking

Some cookies on our website may be set by third parties such as analytics providers, social media platforms, and advertising partners. These third parties farm cookie technologies to collect information across multiple sites, creating topic interest profiles used to display targeted advertising or measure campaign performance. We do not control the use or policies of these cookies, but we encourage you to review the privacy policies of these providers for more details.

Your Cookie Choices and Control

You have the right to manage your cookie preferences and control what information is collected:

  • At any time, you can adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies. However, disabling essential cookies may affect the usability and security of our site.
  • Many browsers offer 'do not track' settings; while we respect these requests, functionality may be limited.
  • Popup banners, like the one at the bottom of this page, provide a convenient interface to accept or decline non-essential cookies.

How to Manage Cookies in Your Browser

Each browser manages cookies differently, but general steps include:

  • Access browser settings or preferences.
  • Locate the cookies or site data section.
  • Choose options to clear existing cookies, block third-party cookies, or disable cookies entirely.
  • Consult browser help pages for detailed, browser-specific instructions.

Cookie Expiry and Data Retention

The duration cookies remain on your device varies:

  • Session cookies expire when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies remain until they expire, which can range from days to years, or until manually deleted.
  • We retain the collected anonymous data following applicable legal and policy requirements to enhance our services responsibly.

The Role of Cookies in Security

Cookies also contribute to website security by helping detect fraudulent activity, securing user sessions, and preventing unauthorized access. Our security systems utilize cookies to enforce a safe browsing environment while balancing privacy considerations.

Impacts of Declining Cookies

If you choose to decline cookies (apart from strictly necessary ones), certain features may be limited or unavailable. These could include:

  • Logging into your account automatically.
  • Personalized content and saved preferences.
  • Participation in surveys or promotional offers.
  • Effective social media sharing.

We encourage you to review your choices regularly to ensure the best experience aligned with your privacy preferences.

Cookies on Mobile Devices and Apps

When accessing our services via mobile devices or apps, similar tracking technologies may be used, subject to platform and app store policies. We strive for consistent privacy practices across all access methods.

Children's Cookies and Parental Controls

Our website does not knowingly target or collect data from individuals under the age of 16. If you are a parent or guardian, you can manage cookie settings or monitor activity through your child's devices and browsers to maintain a safe online environment.
